Passionate team

We're a lot more than a group of environmentalists. We're a team of volunteers that is committed to helping the environment and the communities around us.

With our efforts and with the help of other organizations and communities around the state, we've seen a significant improvement when it comes to pollution and laws to protect the only plant that we have.

We couldn't be happier after all the positive work that has been conducted by Wicker park over the past 2 decades.

Our projects

Our main focus has always been the wicker park, this park is not only located in Buffalo, but most of our team members has a strong connection with this park. Most of us have lived in Buffalo all our lives, and we fell very strongly about preserving our city's environment.

Wicker Park - Protecting the Wild Life

Because the Wicker park is located inthe middle of the city, there aren't that many species. however, it's very important that we protect the few that still inhabit among us. We work very hard on feeding and providing shelter to diffenent bird and duck specied.

Wicker Park - Cleaning and Awareness

Unfortunately, a lot of people don't respect public and green areas. But if none of us take any action to prevent our living areas to become more and more polluted, we'll just live in a dirty and unhealthy environment. We're also working on bringing awareness to different issues related to the environmrnt, and we've seen a clear improvement (in terms of pollution in our parks) over the past years.
